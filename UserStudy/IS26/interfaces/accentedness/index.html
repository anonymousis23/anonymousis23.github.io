Audio Accentedness Evaluation

In the question set 1 to 16, a list of recordings are organized in rows. In each row, you will see an audio clip. Please listen to the recording and rate its degree of foreign accentedness. The native accent in this task is General American.

  • There are nine (9) levels of accentedness as shown in the tables below. "|" means that the degree of accentedness is between two levels. For example, the "|" between "No foreign accent" and "Some foreign accent" means that the accentedness level is more than "No foreign accent" but less than "Some foreign accent"
  • Try to ignore the speaking rate and audio quality (noises, distortions). Please focus only on the accent of the speaker
  • You may listen to each clip more than once if needed; just click the play button to replay.
  • Your responses will be manually screened to ensure that you made a serious effort to rate the recordings. If your answers are determined to be random or contain inconsistent answers, the task may be rejected and you may not receive payment
  • Please perform these tasks in a quiet place using a pair of good headphones. It is recommended that you set the volume a bit higher than normal for these tasks.
  • If you are having trouble viewing or listening to the sound clips, please try completing the test in Google Chrome.
  • Please make sure that you've finished all the questions before submitting.

In each question, please rate the degree of foreign accentedness in the recording. The native accent in this task is General American.
