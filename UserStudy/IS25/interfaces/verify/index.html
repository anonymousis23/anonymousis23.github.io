Speaker Quality/Verification Test

Below is a list of recordings organized in rows. In each row, you will see two clips: A & B. Please listen to the two recordings, and then decide whether the audio quality of clip A is better or worse than clip B. Also, decide whether the voices in the two clips are from the same or different speakers.

  • When rating the quality, please ignore the difference in voice characteristics of the speaker and instead focus on the audio quality (e.g., noises, distortions) of the recording.
  • Similarily, when rating whether speakers are same or different, please ignore the audio quality (e.g., noises, distortions) and instead focus on the voice characteristics (e.g., age, gender, pitch, timbre) of the speaker.
  • You may listen to a sound more than once if needed; just click the play button to replay.
  • Your responses will be manually screened to ensure that you made a serious effort to rate the recordings. If your answers are determined to be random or contain inconsistent answers, the task may be rejected and you may not receive payment.
  • Please perform these tasks in a quiet place using a pair of good headphones. It is recommended that you set the volume a bit higher than normal for these tasks.
  • If you are having trouble viewing or listening to the sound clips, please try completing the test in Google Chrome.
  • Please make sure that you've finished all the questions before submitting.

In each question, answer whether the two audios in clip A and clip B are the same or different speakers. Also rate the relative quality of the clips.
